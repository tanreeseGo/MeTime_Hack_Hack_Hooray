Effects of not enough Sleep

When you feel tired during the day, this is an indication that you don't get enough nightly sleep. You can also have more mistakes, irritability and even forgetfulness. The lack of adequate sleep can also lead to longer-term health effects.It will gradually get worse as well.So please do not let your sleep get away!

Fast Facts

Humans spend 1/3 of their life sleeping

On average it’s around a third, which is quite a lot when you think about it.

Ideally, falling asleep at night should take you 10-15 minutes

If it takes you less than five minutes, chances are, you’re sleep deprived.

Humans are the only mammals that willingly delay sleep
